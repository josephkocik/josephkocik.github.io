hello, world

I am a computer engineer studying as a master's student at the University of Pittsburgh. During my undergrad, also at Pitt, I started as an undergraduate researcher at the National Science Foundation Center for Space, High-performance, and Resilient Computing (NSF SHREC), and am continuing with SHREC for my MS research. I have previously interned with the NASA Johnson Space Center doing computer networks research and at Collins Aerospace doing low level software and FPGA support.

my interests

  • fpgas and embedded design

    I work with FPGA design and software support for SoCs (System on a Chip), both at SHREC and at Collins Aerospace.

  • computer networks

    I worked with Time-Triggered Ethernet network testing and design both at the SHREC and at NASA JSC.

  • computer languages

    In my spare time I enjoy experimenting with programming languages. I enjoy new and obscure languages that use different paradigms. Recently this has been the ML family.
